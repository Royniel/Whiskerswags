Our furry companions are eagerly waiting for their forever homes, each with a unique personality and heartwarming story to share. At Whiskers & Wags, we are passionately dedicated to promoting animal welfare and fostering strong, loving bonds between pets and their owners. By adopting a friend, you open your heart to the unconditional love and companionship they offer. Together, we can make a meaningful difference in the lives of these deserving animals, creating brighter futures filled with joy, laughter, and loyalty. Join us in this noble journey of compassion and commitment—let's give our furry friends the loving homes they deserve!

How to apply for a Pet

1. Choose Pet

Choose the pet you want to adopt and check the details such as age, health status, and ask all those questions you have so that this experience is successful and wonderful.

2. Application Form

Fill in the complete information in the adoption process, remember that this step is the most important of all.

3. Take Pet Home

Take your pet home with you and start a new phase of your life accompanied by a loyal friend who will always be there for you.

FAQ's About Our Services

To adopt a pet from Paws&Tails, you can visit our website or contact us directly. We'll guide you through the adoption process and help you find the perfect pet for your home..

The requirements for pet adoption may vary, but typically include providing a loving and stable home environment, ensuring proper care and nutrition for the pet, and adhering to any specific adoption policies set by Paws&Tails

The time it takes to complete the pet adoption process can vary depending on factors such as the availability of the desired pet, completion of required paperwork, and any necessary home visits. Generally, the process can take anywhere from a few days to a couple of weeks. Our team at Paws&Tails is dedicated to making the process as efficient and smooth as possible for both the adopter and the pet.
